# Land Resources
* Land is the most important natural resource available.
* It supports natural vegetation, wildlife, human life, economic activities, transportation and communication systems.
* Land however, is a finite asset. In other words, it is a limited resource which is why it is important to use it for various purposes with careful planning.

## Relief Features
1. 43% = Plains (Facilities for agriculture and industry)
2. 30% = Mountains (Provides perennial flow rivers, facilities for tourism)
3. 27% = Plateau (Reserves of minerals and fossil fuels)
